Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15th May 2026

1. Introduction

Birdie’s Review (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is an educational mobile application designed to provide NCLEX-style revision questions and learning support. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is handled when you use our app.

2. Data Collection Approach (Privacy-First Design)

Birdie’s Review is built using a minimal data collection model.

We DO NOT collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • User accounts or login credentials

You can use the app without registering or identifying yourself.

3. Information We May Process

a) Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect limited, non-identifiable data such as:

  • App performance metrics
  • Feature usage (e.g., quizzes attempted)
  • General interaction patterns

This data:

  • Cannot identify you personally
  • Is used only to improve app functionality

b) Device-Level Data (via Third Parties)

Birdie’s Review uses third-party services that may process limited technical data, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address (used for connectivity and security)
  • Advertising identifiers

This data is handled by third parties and is not used by us to identify individual users.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads in order to keep the app free.

Important:

  • We use non-personalized ads only
  • Ads are based on app content and general context

What this means:

  • No behavioral tracking
  • No cross-app profiling
  • No targeted advertising based on personal interests

AdMob may still process limited technical data (such as device information and IP address) to serve ads. For more information, see:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Legal Basis for Data Use

We process limited data based on:

  • Legitimate interest (to operate and improve the app)
  • User awareness and transparency (via this policy)

Where required, we request user acknowledgment before showing ads.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Secure connections (HTTPS)
  • Minimal data storage practices
  • No centralized storage of personal user profiles

Where possible, user progress may be stored locally on your device.

7. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade user data.

We only share limited data with:

  • Advertising providers (e.g., AdMob)
  • Technical service providers (for app functionality)

8. Children’s Privacy

Birdie’s Review is intended for educational use and may be accessed by students. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If any personal data is inadvertently collected, it will be deleted upon request.

9. Your Rights

You may have rights to:

  • Request information about data processing
  • Object to certain data use
  • Request deletion of identifiable data (if applicable)

Since we do not collect personal identifiers, fulfilling requests may be limited.

10. Third-Party Links and Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date.
